# Wasmol — Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Friends and family of the owner, referred by word of mouth. They already have some trust and a personal connection. They visit the site to see what services are offered, get a feel for quality, and find a way to reach out. The job-to-be-done is simple: confirm this is legit, see the work, and contact quickly.

### Brand Personality
Sharp · Sleek · Personal. The tone is confident but approachable — not corporate. It's a skilled individual who takes pride in their work. Think: a craftsperson with a clean workshop, not a chain service center.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark mode primary, with light mode support via system preference. The site should feel like a premium product without being cold or unapproachable. Reference: high-end automotive photography — dramatic contrast, deep blacks, sharp details. Anti-reference: generic SaaS landing pages, AI-template aesthetics (cyan gradients, glassmorphism, identical card grids).

### Design Principles
1. **Craft over template** — Every detail should feel intentional, not auto-generated.
2. **Confidence through restraint** — Say less, mean more. Silence is part of the design.
3. **Human at the center** — This is a person's work, not a product. The design should feel personal.
4. **Accessibility by default** — WCAG AA contrast ratios, keyboard navigable, meaningful without color.
5. **Mobile-first** — Friends viewing on phones is the primary context.
